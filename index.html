Here are my dogs. Shadow tends to shrug away from a lot of attention. Spot is active. If you try to pet him, he's going to want to play, and he'll start running. Play nicely with my dogs, please.

I used an event to hide my original image when the user clicks the image, thus displaying the same one with a lower opacity. It's to show you that this floof gets shy.
